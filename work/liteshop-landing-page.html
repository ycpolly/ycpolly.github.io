WEB DESIGN, 2017

LiteShop 
Landing Page

LiteShop is a web-based e-commerce solution for small business to easily build a single-page website for purchasing core products. In 2017, we launched the new “enhanced content” function, which enables merchants to increase sales by incorporating their visually rich brand design into the the product detail page.

The Challenge

Optimize landing page conversion rate

In the early stage of LiteShop, we created a simple one-page checkout as a Minimum Viable Product service suitable even for merchants who only have one item to sell.

With more merchants running Facebook mobile ads, the enhanced one-page checkout has become more important, as it improves the customer experience and effectively boost sales.

In order to attract potential customers, we started to redesign landing page to showcase this new feature.

My Role: UI Designer
Deliverables: High-Fidelity Mockups, Slim and Sass.

Goal

Convert site visitors into customers

The new landing page highlights key features by delivering explicit messages, imagery and animation, telling visitors what they can expect from using LiteShop’s enhanced content function.

In order to generate maximum users, we simplified our registration process with minimal steps. According to the data tracking, the bounce rate has since decreased by 20%.
