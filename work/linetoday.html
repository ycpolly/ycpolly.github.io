WEB UI DESIGN, 2019-NOW

LINE TODAY

LINE TODAY is a global content portal operating in 3 countries with over 18 million monthly active users, aiming to provide users with the informative, reliable and personalized information they need in their daily lives.

The Challenge

Users appreciate fresh content

LINE TODAY delivers quality information to users, but due to the rigid structure of the previous version, users have a hard time finding the latest content and what they need. In addition, we must increase the diversity and inclusion of the design to meet the various habits of international users.

Lack of flexibility

When there is an event, hot story or breaking news, operators can not easily reorder modules right away to let users see the latest content.

Little details matter

  1. The previous design was less readable and distract the users because news images often had overlay text.
  2. The previous aspect ratio was unable to display the entire thumbnail, reducing the integrity and credibility of the photo.

My Role: UI Designer
Deliverables: High-fidelity mockups, prototypes, spec, guideline and design system.

Goal

Keeping the content up to date and engaging

Users read news in a fragmented way. To attract attention and increase stickiness, the flexibility of the structure needs to be improved, allowing operators to reorder modules based on breaking news or trending topics, ultimately allowing users to see the latest content anytime, anywhere.

  • Upgrade to a modular level to make the framework more flexible and maintainable.
  • Build a modular design system to ensure usability and meet cross-cultural user preferences.
  • Create a better reading experience by optimizing readability and offering a dark mode.
Design Solution

Ease of Communication

We provide a variety of header and body styles under the modular structure, allowing operators to form eye-catching modules based on diverse content to meet the preferences of cross-cultural users, improve product experience and monetization.

Design Solution

Keep users engaged

In addition to daily news, we continue to roll out new features like Fortune and Quiz based on user needs in local markets to increase user engagement, satisfaction and retention.
