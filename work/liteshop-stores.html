WEB UI DESIGN, 2018

LiteShop Stores

LiteShop is a web-based e-commerce solution for small business to easily build a single-page website for purchasing core products. In 2018, we launched the "Stores" feature for merchants to tailor their store page by selecting and grouping products into collections. This page allows customers to easily find things by category.

The Challenge

Merchants need a home page

In contrast to other well-rounded e-commerce platforms, LiteShop’s simplicity provides merchants the essential elements for an one-page checkout worry free.

As the business grows, 40% of merchants said they need an appropriate method to showcase all products in one place. The aim is to boost sales and optimize the online visibility by connecting their own domain.

My Role: UI Designer
Deliverables: High-Fidelity Mockups, Slim and Sass.

Goal

To create pleasant mobile shopping experience

Once the basic e-commerce functions have become stable, we began to develop the “stores” feature based on the concept of a “select shop”.

Nowadays more than 50% of internet shopping is done through a mobile device, it is important for the web design to be mobile friendly.

Additionally, most of our merchants sell products via Facebook and Instant Messaging applications, our mobile friendly design can therefore increase conversion rate.

Design Solution

Display products by collection

Merchants can hand pick suitable products and create their own collections.

Design Solution

Prominent banner placement

Prominent banner position can maximise the attention drawn to flash sales or new arrivals.
